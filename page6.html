Miraculous Ladybug

Created by Thomas Astruc in 2015, Miraculous Ladybug follows two protagonists who navigate maintaining their secret identities and personal lives. Heroes Ladybug and Chat Noir fight to protect Paris from akumatized villains, vulnerable people who are taken advantage of by the main villain when showing a negative emotion. While being a show centered around a duo, Ladybug's personal and superhero life shows how confidence and security can change a person. Ladybug also encourages former villains to change from their mistakes and find other ways to cope with strong and painful emotions. The show covers many famous landmarks in Paris and utilizes magical talismen known as Miraculous to be given to different holders.

Impact

Spanning multiple movies and seasons, this cartoon has become another prominent romance magical girl show. Audiences are captivated by the focus on keeping secret identities hidden and the use of real places like Paris and New York. The comedic aspects of the show and world-building have proved effective for impacting audiences. Along with the romantic tropes being popular with its main audience, the show being so involved in showing the city its based in has encouraged fans to visit Paris. The longevity of the show and the way it keeps audiences invested is a testament to its writing and world-building.
